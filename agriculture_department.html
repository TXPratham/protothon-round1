Department of Agriculture

Support for farmers, agricultural growth, research, and resource development. Apply for relevant services and track agricultural complaints easily.

Application Requests

PM-Kisan Registration

Applying for the annual ₹6,000 direct income support (paid in three installments).

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Applying for a special low-interest bank loan to buy seeds, fertilizers, or even dairy equipment.

Crop Insurance (PMFBY)

Applying to protect your crop against natural calamities like droughts, unseasonal rain, or pest attacks.

Farm Machinery Subsidy

Applying for a 50%–80% discount on tractors, harvesters, or specialized tools like rotavators (under the SMAM scheme).

e-NAM Registration

Registering as a seller on the National Agriculture Market to sell your produce online to buyers across India, bypassing local middlemen.

Common Complaints

Claim Rejection

Reporting when an insurance company refuses to pay for crop loss after a disaster.

Fertilizer Shortage/Overpricing

Reporting local shops that are hoarding "Urea" or selling it above the printed price (MRP).

Seed Failure

Reporting if the "certified" seeds you bought from a government center failed to germinate.

PM-Kisan Installment Issues

Complaining if your bank account hasn't received the latest government payment due to "Aadhaar seeding" or technical errors.

Kisan Call Center

(1800-180-1551) A 24/7 toll-free number where you can lodge any technical or administrative grievance in your local language.
