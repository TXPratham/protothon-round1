Electricity Department

Manage your electricity connections, apply for relevant applications and services, or submit complaints safely and reliably.

Application Requests

New Connection (LT/HT)

Applying for a new electricity meter for a house (Low Tension) or factory (High Tension).

Load Change

Requesting to increase (or decrease) the kilowatt capacity of your connection (e.g., if you install multiple ACs).

Name Transfer

Changing the name on the electricity bill after buying a property.

Tariff Change

Shifting from "Domestic" to "Commercial" if you start a shop in your house.

Common Complaints

Billing Issues

Reporting an abnormally high bill or a faulty meter.

Power Outage

Reporting a local transformer failure or a snapped wire.

Voltage Fluctuations

Reporting abnormal voltage levels or fluctuations affecting appliances.
