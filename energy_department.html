Department of Energy / Power

Manage related energy grids, utility policies, and sustainable resources. Apply for new connections, upgrades, or report outages and issues.

Application Requests

New Service Connection (NSC)

Applying for a new electricity meter for a newly built house or shop.

Load Enhancement

If you add heavy appliances like 3-4 ACs or an EV charger, you must apply to increase your sanctioned "kilowatt" (kW) load to avoid fines.

Solar Rooftop Net-Metering

Under schemes like PM-Surya Ghar, you apply here to install solar panels and send excess power back to the grid in exchange for a lower bill.

Temporary Connection

A short-term power line for construction sites, wedding halls, or festivals.

Category Change

Changing your connection type from "Domestic" to "Commercial" (if you start a business) or "Agricultural."

Common Complaints

Billing Errors

Reporting "Average Billing" (when the meter isn't read) or "Burnt Meter" issues where the reading is frozen.

Power Failure / Outage

Reporting local transformer bursts or long hours of unscheduled power cuts.

Voltage Issues

High voltage (which can blow up your TV/Fridge) or low voltage (dim lights).

Dangerous Infrastructure

Reporting a leaning electric pole or a dangling wire that could be a safety hazard.

Corruption / Vigilance

Reporting illegal "hooking" (power theft) or bribery demands from department staff.
