Department of Environment and Climate Change

Monitoring ecological health, combating climate change, and reducing pollution. Apply for Green Clearances through the PARIVESH portal and submit environmental complaints easily.

Application Requests

Environmental Clearance (EC)

Mandatory for large-scale projects like mining, thermal power plants, or large real estate townships. It proves the project won't destroy the local environment.

Consent to Establish (CTE)

Before you start building a factory, you need this "NOC" from the Pollution Control Board.

Consent to Operate (CTO)

Once the factory is built, you apply for this to prove your pollution control machines are actually working.

E-Waste & Plastic Waste Authorization

If you start a business that recycles old electronics or manufactures plastic, you must register here.

Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ) Clearance

If you want to build anything near the sea (within 500 meters of the high tide line), you need this special permit.

Common Complaints

Industrial Pollution

Reporting a factory that is releasing black smoke at night or dumping colored chemicals into a nearby stream.

Noise Pollution

Reporting the use of high-decibel loudspeakers or construction noise beyond 10:00 PM.

Waste Burning

Reporting the open burning of garbage or plastic, which releases toxic fumes.

Lake Encroachment

Reporting instances where a water body is being filled with debris to create land for buildings.

Hazardous Waste Dumping

Reporting the illegal dumping of medical waste or chemical drums in open fields.
