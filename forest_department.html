Forest Department

Manage permissions and compensations related to forestry and wildlife, or submit complaints safely and reliably.

Common Applications

Tree Cutting Permission

In many areas, you need a permit even to cut a tree on your own private property.

Timber Transit Pass

A permit required to transport wood from one location to another.

Wildlife Compensation

Applying for money if a wild animal (like a leopard or elephant) damages your crops or livestock.

Film Shooting / Research

Permission to enter protected areas for professional work.

Common Complaints

Forest Fire

Reporting a fire in nearby woods.

Poaching/Smuggling

Reporting illegal hunting or wood theft (like Sandalwood).

Animal Menace

Reporting wild animals entering human settlements.
