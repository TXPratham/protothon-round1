Application Requests

DigiLocker Account

Applying to fetch and store digital versions of your Marksheets, RC, and Insurance.

Domain Registration

If you are a local government body or school, you apply here to get an official government (.gov.in) web address.

Digital Signature (DSC)

Applying for an electronic signature used by business owners and professionals to sign legal documents online.

E-Pramaan

Applying for a single-sign-on ID that lets you log into hundreds of different government websites with one password.

Common Complaints

Cyber Crime / Fraud

Reporting online financial scams, identity theft, or social media hacking through the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal.

Website Downtime

Reporting when a critical service (like a scholarship portal) is crashing or not loading.

Data Errors

Reporting when your digital certificate (like a Covid vaccine certificate or Aadhaar) has a technical glitch or shows the wrong photo.

Privacy Violations

Reporting the misuse of your personal data by any online platform.
