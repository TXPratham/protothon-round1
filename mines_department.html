Department of Mines and Geology

Mineral exploration, responsible mining regulations, and geological surveying. Apply for relevant services and track mining complaints easily.

Application Requests

Mining Lease (ML) / Quarry Lease (QL)

Applying for the legal right to extract "Major Minerals" (like Coal or Iron) or "Minor Minerals" (like Building Stone, Gravel, or Clay) from a specific plot of land.

Reconnaissance Permit (RP) / Prospecting License (PL)

If you aren't ready to mine yet but want permission to "search" or "survey" an area for minerals.

Transit Pass (E-Permit)

Every truck carrying minerals must have a digital pass. You apply for these in bulk to legally transport sand, stones, or ore from the mine to a buyer.

Dealer’s License

If you want to open a "Stockyard" or a shop to sell minerals (like a granite showroom or a sand yard), you need this permit.

NOC for Land Conversion

If you have land that has minerals, you may need an NOC from this department before you can use it for something else, like building a factory.

Common Complaints

Illegal Mining

Reporting unauthorized digging in riverbeds (sand) or hillsides (stones) that damages the environment.

Illegal Transportation

Reporting trucks that are moving minerals at night without valid digital transit passes.

Blasting Nuisance

Complaining about nearby quarries using high-intensity explosives that cause cracks in residential houses or excessive dust.

Royalty Theft

Reporting instances where a mine owner is extracting more than the permitted quota to avoid paying taxes (Royalty) to the government.
