Department of Revenue

Manage land records, apply for relevant certificates and property documents, or submit complaints safely and reliably.

Common Applications

Certificates

Applying for Caste, Income, Domicile (Residence), or EWS certificates.

Mutation (Dakhil Kharij)

Updating the government records to show you are the new owner after buying land.

Land Survey

Asking a government surveyor to measure your plot boundaries.

Pattas

Applying for a legal land allotment or ownership document.

Common Complaints

Encroachment

Reporting that a neighbor has illegally occupied your land or a public path.

Record Errors

Reporting a spelling mistake or wrong survey number in your land records (like the RTC/Pahani).
