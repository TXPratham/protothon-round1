Road Department

Manage infrastructure development, road permissions, and maintenance. Apply for relevant permissions or submit complaints easily.

Application Requests

Road Cutting Permission

If you are building a house and need to dig across a public road to lay a water pipe or sewage line, you must apply for this and pay a "restoration fee."

Access/Driveway Permission

If you are opening a petrol pump, mall, or large apartment, you need a permit to create an entry/exit point onto a main highway.

No Objection Certificate (NOC)

Required for building any structure within a certain distance of a highway (to allow for future road widening).

Contractor Registration

For local businesses to bid on government tenders to build or repair roads.

Common Complaints

Potholes & Surface Wear

Reporting "black spots" or broken patches that cause accidents or damage vehicles.

Waterlogging

Complaining when a road turns into a pond because the department forgot to clear the side drains.

Missing Signage/Lights

Reporting broken streetlights or the lack of "Speed Breaker Ahead" signs in dangerous areas.

Illegal Speed Breakers

Reporting unauthorized humps built by local residents that don't follow safety standards.

Encroachment

Reporting shops or walls that have extended onto the public footpath or road shoulder.
