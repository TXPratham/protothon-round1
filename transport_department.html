Department of Transport

Provide efficient public transit solutions and oversee civil transport regulations. Apply for relevant services like licenses or submit complaints safely.

Application Requests

Learner's & Driving License (LL/DL)

Applying for your first license, renewing an expired one, or getting an International Driving Permit.

New Vehicle Registration

Getting your "RC" (Registration Certificate) for a new car or bike.

Transfer of Ownership

When you sell your vehicle, you apply here to legally move the ownership to the buyer.

Fitness Certificate (FC)

Required for commercial vehicles (and private ones after 15 years) to prove the vehicle is still safe to drive.

No Objection Certificate (NOC)

Needed if you are moving your vehicle to a different state permanently.

Common Complaints

Traffic Violations

Reporting dangerous driving, illegal parking, or vehicles with modified (loud) silencers.

Overcharging/Corruption

Reporting middlemen (agents) or staff demanding extra fees for services that are legally cheaper.

E-Challan Disputes

If you receive a wrong traffic fine on your phone, you apply here to contest it.

Pollution Violations

Reporting "smoking" vehicles that clearly exceed emission limits.

Lost Documents

Applying for a duplicate RC or DL if yours is lost or stolen.
