Water Department

Manage water utility services, billing, and conservation. Apply for connections or submit complaints safely and reliably.

Application Requests

New Water Connection

To get a fresh pipe connection from the main line to your new house.

Meter Installation/Replacement

If your old meter is broken or you want to install a digital one.

Sewerage Connection

In many cities, the water and sewage departments are merged; you apply here to link your house to the city's underground drainage.

Tanker Request

In areas with water scarcity, you can apply for a government water tanker to be sent to your locality for a wedding or during a dry spell.

Common Complaints

Contaminated Water

If the water coming out of your tap is muddy, smelly, or yellow.

Pipeline Leakage

Reporting a major "road-burst" where water is wasting onto the street.

Low Water Pressure

When the water doesn't have enough force to reach your overhead tank.

Billing Errors

If you receive a "ghost bill" (an abnormally high amount) despite low usage.

Illegal Tapping

Reporting neighbors who use heavy suction motors (tullu pumps) directly on the main line, which reduces water for everyone else.
