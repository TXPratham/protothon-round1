Department of Water Resources

Manage water conservation, irrigation water release, and groundwater monitoring. Apply for relevant permissions or submit complaints easily.

Application Requests

Borewell Permission

In many states, you must apply to this department for a permit before digging a deep borewell, especially in "over-exploited" zones.

Irrigation Water Release

Farmers apply for a "Shejpali" or water quota to have canal water released into their fields during the cropping season.

Industrial Water Allotment

Large factories apply for permission to draw a specific volume of water from a nearby river or dam.

Sand Mining NOC

If someone wants to mine sand from a riverbed, they need a No Objection Certificate from this department to ensure it won't damage the river's flow.

Common Complaints

Canal Breach

Reporting a crack or collapse in a canal wall that is flooding nearby farms.

River Pollution

Reporting a factory or city drain that is dumping untreated sewage directly into a river.

Illegal Damming

Reporting if someone has built an unauthorized check-dam or obstruction on a natural stream.

Encroachment on Lake Beds

Reporting builders or individuals filling up old ponds or lakes to build houses.

Groundwater Theft

Reporting commercial water tankers illegally sucking up groundwater in residential areas.
